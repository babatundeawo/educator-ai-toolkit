Which one do you need this week?

Each card below is a self-contained, step-by-step guide. Pick one and follow it from top to bottom, no extra reading required.

Exam, Marking Guide & Revision File Generator

Type a term, subject and class. Get a 70-mark exam paper with its own marking guide, or a revision file of notes and answered practice questions.

  • Section A: 30 objective questions, Section B: theory, 70 marks total
  • Or a self-study revision file in one document
Start this guide →

Weekly Lesson Note Generator

Type a subject, class and week number. Get a complete lesson note booklet for every period that week, dates worked out from your state's calendar.

  • 3 periods a week for most subjects, 4 for Maths & English
  • Works from full e-notes, or a topic list alone
Start this guide →

The Exam Generator isn't tailored to English Language or Mathematics yet. Every other subject is fully supported.

Before you start

What you'll need

Device

A phone or a computer. Nothing else.

Connection

Internet, only while setting up or generating.

Materials

Your e-notes (all 3 terms), or a topic list if you don't have e-notes.
