Before the tools

Get Claude running in about 15 minutes

Two steps, done once, then never again. After this, go to either tool's guide to finish setup.

Step 1 of 3

1

Download Claude and sign up

Free, takes about 2 minutes
DeviceWhere
Browserclaude.ai, no install needed
iPhone / iPadApp Store
AndroidPlay Store
Laptop / Desktopclaude.com/download

Look for the app by Anthropic (white background, orange logo). Open it and tap Sign Up, fastest is "Continue with Google," or use your email and confirm the verification link Claude sends you.

2

Create a Project

One per tool, holds all your subjects and classes

Make one Project per tool, e.g. "Exam & Revision Generator" and "Lesson Note Generator." Each one holds materials for every subject and class you teach, so you only ever set it up once.

Phone app
  1. Open the menu (☰) → Projects
  2. Tap New Project
  3. Name it, tap Create
Computer, claude.ai
  1. Projects in the sidebar
  2. Click New Project
  3. Name it, click Create
3

Choose your tool

Two options, pick the one you need this week

The instruction text and files you upload differ by tool. Pick one, it walks you through the rest, step by step.

Tool 1

Exam, Marking Guide & Revision File Generator →

Paste the exam instruction, upload your term e-notes.

 Tool 2

Weekly Lesson Note Generator →

Paste the lesson note instruction, upload e-notes or topic lists.

Always copy the instruction text from this website, never type it from memory. The exact wording is what makes the formatting come out right every time.

Don't share your Claude login with anyone, treat it like your email password.
